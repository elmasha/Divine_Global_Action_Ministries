About Divine Global Action Ministries

Divine Global Action Ministries,  was first started up by Apostle Angela Reed , The Spirit of Living God spoke to Apostle Angela Reed in her bedroom on day of her birthday. Told her to Start up Church and Foundation helping his people around world.  APOSTLE ANGELA ask Spirit of  Living  God  who was his people.  Spirit of Living God told her his people  was poor  and people  around  world. After that God told  her registered  Church  and  Foundation  and  do it quickly  for  God said  he be sending  her  into  many  Nations  Preaching  Gospel  and  helping  his people.  And  that God would  give  het many  Nations.  And  that  she  be called  mother of  Nations.  Apostle Angela prayed for name of Church and God gave her Divine Global Action Ministries,  And  Foundation name OHIRIANGELHANDS Foundation organization.  She soon after registered Church and Foundation in Rhode Island United States of America.  Apostle Angela started with 2 members her and her son Quinton Mckinnon as Evangelist.  She went on Preaching Gospel winning soul for Christ Jesus.  Soon after meeting her husband Apostle Martins Ohiri, Ordaining him under Divine Global Action Ministries and she became then known as Apostle Angela Ohiri. Apostle Angela Ohiri and Apostle Martins Ohiri went on winning Soul for Jesus Christ Lord Bless them have over 100 Pastors and branches around world under them Divine Divine Global is a International Ministry well known now. .Divine Global Action Ministries has grown in record numbers with many members. The OHIRIANGELHANDS Foundation has been helping orphanages and Needy families and doing outreach Programs, Free clinics, giving out free eye glasses and foods to God people as Spirit of Living God had spoken.  Divine Global Action Ministries is a Fertile Ground Anointed Prophetic ,Healing Ministries and known around world.. Apostles Angela Ohiri travel in different countries along aside her husband Apostle Martins Ohiri fullfilling vision God gave them.
